Porting Meetingmatch iOS app to android

Business social app which help attendees of large event to find interesting contacts before entering event and schedule useful meetings. Main features: registration with custom survey, tinder-like contact recommendations based on bio and survey answers, chat, search, event info and push notifications.

App was ready in time and successfully used on many events with further improvements.

Check on google play

Environment

Android Studio, Xcode, Shugar ORM, Python, Socket.io

Team Lead. Communication with customer team, discussing and setting milestones, planning work. Help team with most important parts of app. Release the app.
