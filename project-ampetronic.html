Loopworks Measure iOS app

Loopworks Measure combines an iOS phone or tablet App that utilises a self-calibrating Receiver that together become the most accurate, dedicated field strength meter (FSM) currently available. The App and Receiver are supported by the Loopworks online database and reporting tools. See details: http://www.ampetronic.co/Products/testing_equipment/Loopworks-Measure-iOS-App/App

App was successfully released. Future improvements was done without me.
