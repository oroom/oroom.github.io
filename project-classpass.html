Classpass iOS app

Mobile app for fast growing fitness startup working in several countries. The main idea of the app is to provide month plan which give access to a lot of different fitness and sport activities and studios. The app provide search, reviews, suggested classes, favorites and other stuff to make classes search and booking easy.
